From Bean to Cup: Our Coffee Journey

Founded in 2018 by Katherine Müller, Kaffe Kath began as a small roastery with a big dream—to bring exceptional, ethically sourced coffee to the community. What started as a passion project has grown into a beloved destination for coffee enthusiasts.

We travel directly to coffee-growing regions, building relationships with farmers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. This direct trade approach ensures fair compensation for growers while allowing us to select only the finest beans.

Our master roaster carefully develops unique profiles for each batch, highlighting the distinct characteristics of every origin. The result? Coffee that's as distinctive as the lands and people who cultivated it.

Our Coffee Story

Every bean has a journey, every cup has a tale

At Kaffe Kath, we believe coffee is more than a beverage—it's a connection between continents, cultures, and communities. Our beans travel from the misty mountains of Colombia to the sun-drenched hills of Ethiopia, each carrying the unique character of its origin.

We've built direct relationships with small-scale farmers who implement sustainable practices. These partnerships allow us to ensure fair compensation while securing beans of exceptional quality. Our visits to these farms reinforce our commitment to ethical sourcing and help us better understand the craft behind each harvest.

Back in our roastery, we treat each batch as a unique canvas. Light roasts highlight delicate floral and fruity notes, while our medium and dark roasts develop rich chocolate and caramel flavors. The result is a spectrum of profiles to suit every palate.
